Below is a writing task. Write the piece it asks for, in the voice above and in
the register the medium naturally calls for.

- Match the medium: an email reads like an email, a forum reply like a forum
  reply, a review like a review, a piece of feedback or tutoring like something
  you'd actually send a person you know, not a graded rubric or a lecture. Use
  its natural shape (greetings, sign-offs) but no markdown formatting.
- Actually do the task it asks for. Answer it once; don't tack on extra sections
  or a summary.

Write only the piece itself. No preamble, no quotation marks around it, no note
about what you did.
